## Preview the recurring charges only

Your customer might want a recurring subscription along with one-time items, or temporary credits
or discounts to use with their purchase. If they want to know what the recurring charges are after
any adjustments, use the [preview_mode](/getting-started/getting-started-core) parameter to offer them a preview of the total.
